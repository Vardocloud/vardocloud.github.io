# PTE Medical Accommodation Letter Template (İngilizce)
## DEHB/Dikkat Eksikliği için Ek Süre Başvurusu

Bu şablon, Pearson PTE'ye özel düzenleme (extra time, separate room, break) talebi için hazırlanmıştır. Edel'in resmi raporlarına (2016 ve 2017, Ege Üniversitesi Hastanesi) dayanmaktadır.

## Kullanım
1. Köşeli parantez içindeki yerleri kendine göre düzenle
2. Raporları yanına al
3. Pearson PTE "Special Arrangements" formunda "Describe your condition" kısmına yapıştır

## Şablon

**Medical Condition and Request for Test Accommodations**

**Diagnosis:** F90.0 — Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD), predominantly inattentive type

I was diagnosed with ADHD on [Tarih, örn: February 3, 2016] at [Hastane Adı], under the supervision of [Doktor Adı]. The diagnosis was reaffirmed the following year on [İkinci Rapor Tarihi], with a second medical report signed by [sayı] specialists.

Both reports confirm the same ICD code **F90.0 (Activity and Attention Disorder)** and document two consecutive years of medical supervision.

**Treatment History:**
I was prescribed [ilaç isimleri] — a stimulant and a non-stimulant combination — indicating a moderate-to-severe attention disorder requiring long-term pharmacological management.

**How ADHD Affects My Test Performance:**
1. **Reading comprehension difficulty** — My attention drifts while reading, causing me to lose track of sentences. I often have to reread questions multiple times to understand them.
2. **Slow processing speed** — I process information and formulate responses significantly slower than the average test taker.
3. **Impaired memory retrieval** — Under time pressure, I struggle to recall information I have studied, even when I know the material well.

**Accommodations Requested:**
- **Extra time (25%):** To compensate for slow reading and processing speed
- **Separate room:** To minimize distractions and help maintain focus during the test
- **Break(s):** To manage mental fatigue, as sustained concentration requires significantly more effort for me

**Supporting Documents:**
- Medical report from [Hastane] (dated [tarih])
- Medical report from [Hastane] (dated [tarih])
