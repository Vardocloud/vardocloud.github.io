## Common Requirements Across Region

### Standard Documentation
1. **High School Diploma**: Official graduation certificate
2. **Academic Transcripts**: All years of high school (12th grade minimum)
3. **Passport**: Valid international passport
4. **Birth Registration Certificate**: Blue Card (dual citizenship optional)

### Optional/Region-Specific Requirements
1. **Turkish Preparatory Class**: Required for some universities
2. **Turkish Language Proficiency**: Varies by program
3. **Motivation Letter**: Common for international applicants
4. **Recommendation Letters**: Important for some programs
5. **Self-Introduction Letter**: Required by some universities (e.g., Ağrı)

## Unique Regional Features
