## The 6 Skills

| # | Skill | Core Idea |
|---|-------|-----------|
| 1 | **Be the AI Person** | Be the most AI-literate person in your circle. It's a *relative* advantage — you don't need to know everything, just more than those around you |
| 2 | **Taste & Judgment** | Know when to USE AI and when NOT to. As AI gets better, the temptation to trust its first output grows. The real skill is discerning good from bad |
| 3 | **Context Engineering** | Give AI high-quality context to get high-quality output. Goes beyond prompt engineering — it's about building rich, structured context environments |
| 4 | **Iteration Speed** | Being able to tell good from bad isn't enough — you need to reach good FAST. Speed of iteration is the biggest separator between winners and left-behinds |
| 5 | **Build Your Own JARVIS** | Teach your AI to act on what it knows without you having to be the trigger. Like Tony Stark's JARVIS — an always-on, background AI assistant that works autonomously |
| 6 | **Unemployment Insurance** | Create your own unique value that AI can't replicate. Not literal insurance — build something that makes you indispensable regardless of AI disruption |
