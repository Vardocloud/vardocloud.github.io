# APA Article Wiki Conventions

## File Naming
`~/wiki/apa-articles/YYYY-MM-[kisa-baslik].md`

## Frontmatter
```yaml
---
title: "Original APA Title"
created: YYYY-MM-DD
type: apa-article
tags: [psychology, topic1, topic2]
source_url: https://www.apa.org/...
---
```

## Page Structure (Turkish)
1. **Ana Fikir** — 1 sentence essence
2. **Türkçe Özet** — 2-3 paragraphs plain Turkish
3. **Önemli Bulgular** — 3-5 bullet points
4. **Pratik Uygulama** — clinical/real-world use
5. **Anahtar Kelimeler** — 5-10 Turkish keywords

## Bloat Management
| Trigger | Action |
|----------|--------|
| 50+ articles in `apa-articles/` | Archive 10 oldest to `_archive/apa/` |
| 1st of each month | Archive articles >90 days old |
| Every archive | Log in `log.md`, remove from `index.md` |

Active window: ~40-50 articles. Archive preserves everything.

## Edel Learning Package Format
Every article delivered as ONE message:
```
🧠 TITLE | 💡 MAIN IDEA (1 line) | 📖 SUMMARY | 🔑 KEY CONCEPTS | ⭐ KEY POINTS | 🎙️ PODCAST | 🔗 Link
```
Turkish, plain language, explain jargon. "Like explaining to a psychologist friend over coffee."
