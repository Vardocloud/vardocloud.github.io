### Known GPA minimums

#### Exact thresholds from verified sources
1. **Ege**: GPA ≥ 2.50
2. **IEÜ**: Not explicitly stated
3. **Bakırçay**: ALES ≥ 55 standard
4. **Yaşar**: GPA ≥ 2.70
5. **Bursa Uludağ (devlet): GPA ≥ 2.25/4.00** (en düşük eşik! BAŞVURU: 1 Temmuz-26 Ağustos 2026) ✅
6. **DEÜ (devlet):** 29 Haziran 2026 kontenjan açıklanacak

### ALES minimums verified
- **55 EA**: Çoğu vakıf üniversitesi (Altınbaş, Beykoz, Üsküdar vb.)
- **60 EA**: Işık, TOBB ETÜ
- **65 EA**: Edel'in puanı — çoğu üniversite için yeterli
- **70 EA**: Acıbadem, Hacettepe, Ankara — yetersiz ❌
- **Tezsiz programlarda ALES aranmaz**: Altınbaş, Üsküdar, Uludağ

### Fee structure nuances

#### Exact amounts from sources
1. **Ege Üniversitesi**: 1,600,000 TL (KDV dahil) (source: Acıbadem affiliate page)
2. **IEÜ**: 980,000 TL (full), 1,180,606 TL (installment) (source: 2025-2026 Instagram)
3. **Multiple sources report**: 980,000-1,000,000 TL range for similar programs
4. **Tezsiz vs Tezli**: 540,000 TL (tezsiz) vs 980,000 TL (tezli) in some universities

### GPA and ALES requirements minimum

#### Exact thresholds
1. **Ege**: ALES ≥55 (standard), GPA calculation formula used
2. **IEÜ**: Not explicitly stated, but top 2 students get 100% scholarship
3. **BAKİRÇAY**: ALES ≥55 (tezli), specific backgrounds for tezsiz
4. **YAŞAR**: ALES ≥60 (sayısal), YDS ≥75 or equivalent

### YÖK recognition checking

#### Document verification needed
- Yükseköğretim Kurulu (YÖK) diploma denkliği
- Foreign degree equivalency approval
- Both Turkish and international candidates affected

### Language requirements variations

#### Exact requirements
1. **Foreign language exams accepted**
   - YDS / e-YDS / ÜDS / KPDS / YÖKDİL
   - University-level exam scores (100/70 minimum for some)
   - Lise/Üniversite düzeyindeki İngilizce eğitim de kabul görür

2. **University-specific**
   - Ege: English exam (70/100 minimum) +
   - IEÜ: English-medium bachelor's degree (last 3 years) waives exam
   - YÖKAR: e-YDS/ÜDS/KPDS/YÖKDİL ≥50 or equivalent exam

### Missing information scenarios

#### "Net değil" approach
When information unavailable:
- **Fee not on website**: Say "net değil" rather than omitting
- **YÖK denklik**: Check official YÖK recognition process
- **Scholarship status**: Verify if available via university-specific processes

### Quality indicators of reliable sources

#### What makes a source authoritative
1. **Official university websites**: .edu.tr domains, dedicated program pages
2. **PDF documents**: Program-specific fee/requirement PDFs
3. **Direct university announcements**: Program-specific social media posts
4. **Multiple source verification**: Cross-check across 2-3 different sources
